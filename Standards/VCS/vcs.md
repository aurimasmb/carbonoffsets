# Verified Carbon Standard



### Land-Based Projects

One concern with afforestation projects is the loss of carbon stocks due to future deforestation or fire.

There are a few different mechanisms for dealing with this:

* Temporary Credits
* Legal guarantees 
* Financial Insurance
* Buffer Pools
